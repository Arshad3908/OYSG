About Us

Obokun Youth for Sustainable Growth (OYSG) is a dynamic and dedicated youth organization established in Obokun Local Government. Our primary focus is on empowering young people, promoting unity, and fostering the sustainable development of our local government area. We believe that by nurturing the talents and aspirations of our youth, we can create a brighter future for the entire community. At OYSG, we strive to provide a platform for young people to engage in meaningful dialogue, share ideas, and collaborate on projects that address the challenges faced by our local government. Our programs and initiatives cover a wide range of areas, including education, entrepreneurship, environmental conservation, social welfare, and cultural exchange.


Mission: To empower, unite, and develop the youth of Obokun Local Government, fostering sustainable growth and prosperity for the community.


Vision: To create a thriving, equitable, and sustainable Obokun community by harnessing the potential and passion of its youth.


Core Values: Empowerment: We believe in empowering our youth with the knowledge, skills, and resources they need to make a positive impact on their community.

Unity: We foster a sense of unity among our members, encouraging collaboration and cooperation for the greater good of Obokun.

Sustainability: We prioritize sustainable growth and development, ensuring that our actions today will benefit future generations.

Innovation: We promote creativity and innovation, inspiring our youth to think outside the box and develop new solutions to existing problems.

Inclusiveness: We embrace diversity and inclusiveness, ensuring that all young people in Obokun have a voice and an opportunity to contribute to the community's development.

Together, we aim to make Obokun a beacon of hope, growth, and unity, driven by the passion and dedication of our youth. Join us in our mission to build a better future for the people of Obokun Local Government.
